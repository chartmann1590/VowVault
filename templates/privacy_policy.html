Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our Wedding Photo Gallery. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our wedding photo sharing service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe online experience for all our wedding guests.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our service, you may provide:

  • Name: When uploading photos, commenting, or signing the guestbook
  • Photos and Videos: Content you choose to upload to the gallery
  • Messages and Comments: Text you post on the message board or photo comments
  • Location (Optional): If you choose to share it in the guestbook

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information through cookies:

  • User Preferences: Your name preference for future uploads
  • Interaction Data: Photos you've liked (stored anonymously)
  • Session Information: To remember if you've seen the welcome modal

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your uploaded photos and videos in the gallery
  • Show your comments and messages to other guests
  • Remember your preferences for a better user experience
  • Track likes and interactions with content
  • Create a memorable digital keepsake of the wedding celebration

Information Sharing

Public Nature of Content: Please be aware that all photos, videos, messages, and guestbook entries you submit are visible to all users of the gallery. This is designed to create a shared experience for all wedding guests.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The gallery is hosted privately and accessible only to those with the gallery URL.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Upload content voluntarily - participation is entirely optional
  • Choose what name to display with your content
  • Contact the wedding couple to request removal of your content

Children's Privacy

This service is intended for wedding guests of all ages under adult supervision. Parents should monitor their children's use of the gallery.

Cookies

We use browser cookies to enhance your experience. These cookies help us remember your name for future uploads, track which photos you've liked, and remember your preferences for app installation prompts. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit some functionality.

PWA Installation Prompts: We use a cookie to remember when you dismiss the app installation prompt. This cookie expires after 30 days and helps us avoid showing the same prompt repeatedly. The cookie is set with SameSite=Lax for security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request removal of your content, please contact the wedding couple directly or the site administrator.
