How It Works

Photobooth Process:
  1. Guests visit the photobooth page
  2. They take a photo with their camera
  3. Your wedding border is applied automatically
  4. They can download the photo or upload to gallery
  5. Photos appear in the main gallery
Features:
  • Custom wedding border overlay
  • Camera access for photo capture
  • Download or upload to gallery
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Automatic border application
Border Requirements:
  • PNG format with transparency
  • 1280x720px recommended size
  • 16:9 aspect ratio works best
  • Transparent areas for photo placement
